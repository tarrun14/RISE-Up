Overview

The Eco Candles Project by Enactus REC transforms everyday lighting into a means of environmental and social impact. Candles are made with eco-friendly materials like soy wax or recycled wax, reducing the carbon footprint.

By avoiding harmful chemicals and paraffin, we promote conscious consumerism while providing livelihood opportunities to marginalized communities.
